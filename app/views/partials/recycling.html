Bethlehem City Ordinance #3321 States, “All persons shall source separate the designated recyclables generated at commercial, municipal, or institutional establishments and from community activities.” Designated recyclables mean glass, plastic 1-7, aluminum, and steel cans (co-mingled material), and corrugated cardboard.

You can either 1) hire a private hauler to collect your recycling 2) self-haul your recycling collected in your own recycling containers to a recycling processor, or 3) lease recycling containers and/or trailers from the Recycling Bureau and self-haul your recycling to a recycling processor. Regardless of collection method, copies of weight slips must be turned in to the Recycling Bureau with in 2 business days of the close of the event.
